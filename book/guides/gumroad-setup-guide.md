# Gumroad Setup Guide

A step-by-step guide for setting up and selling "AI for the Rest of Us" on Gumroad.

---

## 1. Account Creation

1. Go to [gumroad.com](https://gumroad.com) and sign up with your email address.
2. **Payment setup:** Gumroad uses Stripe Connect for payouts. You will be prompted to connect a bank account so Gumroad can deposit your earnings. Follow the Stripe onboarding flow to link your bank.
3. **Profile setup:** Add your name, a short bio, and a profile picture. This is what buyers see on your Gumroad creator page, so keep it professional and relevant to your book's audience.
4. **Expected timeline:** Your account is ready to use immediately. Payout setup through Stripe takes a few business days to verify, so do this early — don't wait until launch day.

---

## 2. Product Creation

Once your account is set up, create your product:

1. Click **"New Product"** from your Gumroad dashboard.
2. **Product type:** Select "Digital product."
3. **Name:** Enter the full title:
   > AI for the Rest of Us: 12 Things You Need to Know Before Everyone Else Figures It Out
4. **Price:** Set to **$9.99**.
5. **Description:** Paste your sales copy from `book/marketing/gumroad-sales-page.md`. This is the text buyers will see on the product page, so make sure it renders correctly and reads well.
6. **Upload file:** Upload your styled PDF (the final output from the manuscript conversion process). This is the file buyers will receive after purchase.
7. **Cover image:** Upload your book cover — use the same cover image you created for KDP. Gumroad will display this as the product thumbnail.
8. **Product URL:** Customize your Gumroad product URL to something clean and memorable, e.g.:
   ```
   gumroad.com/l/ai-for-the-rest-of-us
   ```

---

## 3. Launch Checklist

Before sharing your product link publicly, verify everything works end to end:

- [ ] **Test purchase:** Use Gumroad's test mode or buy your own product to verify the full purchase flow. Confirm the checkout process, payment confirmation, and delivery all work as expected.
- [ ] **Verify PDF download:** After the test purchase, download the file and open it. Make sure it is the correct version of your PDF — the right content, properly styled, with no missing pages or broken formatting.
- [ ] **Check email receipt:** Verify the post-purchase confirmation email looks right. This is the first thing your buyer sees after paying, so make sure it includes the download link and doesn't look broken.
- [ ] **Discount codes:** You can create discount codes for launch week promotions (e.g., 20% off for early buyers).

  > **CRITICAL WARNING: Do not use public discount codes that reduce the effective price below $4.99 (your KDP list price).**
  >
  > **Why this matters:** Amazon's price-matching policy can automatically lower your KDP price to match the lowest publicly discoverable price for your book. Gumroad's public coupon pages are indexable by search engines, which means any public coupon you create should be assumed visible to Amazon's price-matching crawlers. If Amazon detects a lower price on Gumroad and auto-drops your KDP price to match, you lose control of your pricing on the platform where most of your sales volume likely comes from.
  >
  > **Private/direct-link discount codes** (shared only via email or DM, not on a public coupon page) carry less risk since they are harder for crawlers to discover. However, caution is still advised — treat any discount as potentially discoverable.
  >
  > **Bottom line:** If you run a promotional discount, keep the effective price at or above $4.99 to avoid triggering Amazon's price-match system.

- [ ] **Share the link:** Your product page is ready to share. Post your Gumroad link on social media, in your email newsletter, and anywhere else you reach your audience.
