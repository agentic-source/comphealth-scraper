# AI for the Rest of Us — E-Book Project Spec

## Overview

Create and launch a beginner-friendly e-book titled **"AI for the Rest of Us: 12 Things You Need to Know Before Everyone Else Figures It Out"**, sold on both Amazon KDP and Gumroad with a post-purchase email automation sequence.

## Target Audience

General public / curious adults who keep hearing about AI, automation, and "agentic" in the news and want to understand what it all means in plain language — no technical background assumed.

## Tone

Conversational and friendly. Like a knowledgeable friend explaining things over coffee. Casual, relatable, uses everyday analogies.

## Book Details

- **Length:** ~25,000–35,000 words (~100–140 pages)
- **Format:** DOCX (master), EPUB (Kindle), PDF (Gumroad)

## Chapter Outline

| # | Title | Coverage |
|---|-------|----------|
| 1 | What Even Is AI? | Strip away the sci-fi. What AI actually is in plain language — pattern recognition, not sentient robots. |

| 3 | How to Actually Talk to AI | Prompting basics. How to ask questions that get useful answers. The "garbage in, garbage out" principle. |
| 4 | What AI Is Good At (And Terrible At) | Real strengths and real limitations. Where to trust it, where to double-check. |
| 5 | Automation ≠ AI (But They're Best Friends) | What automation means vs. what AI means. How they work together. Everyday examples. |
| 6 | AI Tools You Can Use Today for Free | Practical walkthrough of free tools anyone can start using right now. |
| 7 | "Will AI Take My Job?" | Honest answer. Which jobs change, which don't, and how to be on the right side of it. |
| 8 | AI at Home: Practical Everyday Uses | Smart home, meal planning, budgeting, travel, health questions — real-life applications. |
| 9 | AI at Work: Even If You're Not "Techy" | Email drafting, meeting summaries, spreadsheet help, writing assist — office-ready use cases. |
| 10 | What Is "Agentic AI" and Why Is Everyone Talking About It? | The newest wave explained simply. AI that does multi-step tasks on its own. |
| 11 | Privacy, Safety & the Stuff Worth Worrying About | What data you're giving away, what's actually risky, and how to protect yourself. |
| 12 | Your 30-Day AI Starter Plan | Actionable daily/weekly challenges to go from "I've never used AI" to confident user. |

## Chapter Structure

Each chapter includes:

- A relatable opening story or scenario
- The core concept in plain language
- A "Try This Now" hands-on exercise
- A "Myth vs. Reality" sidebar
- Key takeaway summary

## Deliverables

| Deliverable | Format | Purpose |
|---|---|---|

### Platform Work (User handles, with step-by-step guides)

- Create Amazon KDP account
- Create Gumroad account
- Design cover in Canva (using provided brief)
- Upload EPUB + cover to KDP and publish
- Upload PDF to Gumroad, paste in sales page copy
- Set up email sequence in Gumroad or ConvertKit free tier

## Distribution & Pricing

| Platform | Price | Creator Take | Buyer Gets |
|----------|-------|-------------|------------|
| Amazon KDP | $4.99 | ~$3.49 (70% royalty) | EPUB on Kindle |
| Gumroad | $9.99 | ~$8.99 (90% after 10% fee) | PDF + 3-email bonus series |

### Why two price points

Gumroad premium is justified by the direct relationship + email bonus content. Amazon's lower price is the impulse-buy entry point with massive discoverability. Gumroad discount codes can be used for launch week to drive early sales.

## Amazon KDP Listing Assets

- Book title + subtitle optimized for Kindle search
- 4,000-character book description with HTML formatting
- 7 backend keywords (hidden search terms for Amazon SEO)
- 2 category recommendations to maximize visibility

## Gumroad Sales Page Assets

- Headline + subheadline
- Bullet-point benefit list (what the reader walks away with)
- "Who this is for" section
- Social proof placeholder (for reviews once available)
- FAQ section (3–4 common objections answered)

## Post-Purchase Email Sequence

| Email | Timing | Purpose |
|-------|--------|---------|
| Welcome | Immediately after purchase | Thank the buyer, set expectations, link to Chapter 6's free tools for an instant win |
| Quick Win | Day 3 | Walk through one specific AI exercise from the book — prove the value |
| What's Next | Day 7 | Ask what they found most useful, tease future content, invite them to share the book |

## Constraints & Notes
